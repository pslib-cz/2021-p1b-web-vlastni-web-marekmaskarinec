AL Wire

The wire module adds wires, which can be used to dynamically alter boards.

Current

When a block sends a current, it instantly travels as a pulse across the whole circuit, toggling the state of every block on the said circuit.

States:

A B
^ v
< >
# *
i d
a s
m n

As opposed to normal blocks, wire blocks donâ€™t change their state upon receiving current. Instead they either create, transport or proccess current.

@

Laser detector. Interacts with Laser the same exact way as a * block does. But unlike *, @ sends pulse over any connected wire when it gets evaluated.
